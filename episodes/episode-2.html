Working theme

Sales work becomes software when the steps are clear enough.

The practical direction: capture the workflow, give AI the right context, let it draft and route work, then review before anything customer-facing ships.

Step 01

Map the sales motion.

Write down the path from lead source to closed deal. Include research, enrichment, outreach, follow-up, CRM updates, and handoff points.

Step 02

Find the repeatable inputs.

Agents work best when they receive stable inputs: call transcripts, account pages, CRM fields, email history, and lead lists.

Step 03

Automate drafts before actions.

Use AI to draft account notes, outreach copy, follow-up tasks, and meeting prep before letting it send or edit anything live.

Step 04

Review the queue daily.

The operator still owns taste, priority, and customer context. AI should reduce the blank page, not remove judgment.
