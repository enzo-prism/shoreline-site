Operator playbook

How to think about tokens as working capital.

Step 01

Classify the task.

Use cheaper or local models for drafts and extraction. Use frontier models for architecture, judgment, hard debugging, and final review.

Step 02

Route by risk.

Low-risk exploration can burn more tokens. Customer-facing, revenue, or database changes need slower review and stronger logs.

Step 03

Preserve context.

Write down what the agent learned, where output landed, and what should be reused next time.

Step 04

Compare tools by workflow fit.

Test Codex, Claude Code, Cursor, and other tools on the same task. Keep the one that creates the least review burden.
